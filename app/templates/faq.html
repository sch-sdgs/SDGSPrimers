How do I change the display properties in MS Internet Explorer?

To display Primers correctly in IE, you may need to change some of your default settings:
  • - Press to display the IE menu
  • - Click "Tools" and then "Compatibility View settings"


  • - In the Compatibility View menu, make sure the "Display intranet sites in compatibility view is unchecked


    • Warning! If Starlims is not in the list; open Starlims as normal, go to the Compatibility View menu as described above and click "Add" to make sure it is still shown in compatibiltiy view

Why can't I login to Primers?

In order to use Primers, your windows username must be stored in the database.
If you are not able to login to Primers, send an email with your windows login name to bioinformatics and someone will add you to the system.

What do I enter under primer position?

This is the position of the primer in the genome. Choose the relevant chromosomes and enter the 5' (left most) position of the primer in the position box.

How do I identify pairs of primers?

When viewing primers in tables you can see a primer is assigned to a pair as the paired icon is displayed next to it:

If you click the name of a primer that is paired the information about the other member of the pair will be displayed on the primer detail page.

You can also view primers as pairs by clicking the "View As Pairs" button on the Primers > View Primers page.
